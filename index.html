Not sure where to start?

We'll help you find
the right fit.

Here's a quick guide to help you understand which of our practices is the best match for your needs.

Choose Insight Into Action Therapy if…

"I need therapy, medication, or mental health support."

You or a loved one is dealing with anxiety, depression, trauma, autism, behavioral challenges, or relationship issues. This is also the right place for medication management and outpatient substance use counseling.

Choose Insight Recovery Centers if…

"I need a structured recovery program for substance use."

You need a higher level of care for substance use — including an Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP), structured group treatment, or our alcohol moderation program.

Not sure?

"I don't know where to begin."

That's completely okay. Use the contact form below and one of our team members will help guide you to the right place — no pressure, no obligation.

Different levels of care

"What's the difference between the two?"

Insight Into Action Therapy offers individual outpatient care for clients of all ages, while Insight Recovery Centers provides structured, intensive programs specifically designed for adults’ substance use recovery and harm reduction.
